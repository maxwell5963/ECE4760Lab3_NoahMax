Project Introduction

In this project, we built a handheld VGA console around the Raspberry Pi Pico to re-create the feel of an 8-bit side-scrolling platformer. We implemented our own tile-map renderer, sprite animations, and collision physics entirely in C, and we offloaded all VGA timing to the Pico’s PIO + DMA.

The result is a playable demo of the first level of Super Mario Bros., complete with scrolling background, enemy AI, and a responsive control scheme.
